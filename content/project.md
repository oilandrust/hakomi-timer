For a few years now, I have been training in Psychotherapy online.
During the training, we have practice sessions in groups of 3 where we take turns being the therapist, client, or observer to practice the skills we are learning.
In each of these sessions, we have to divide the time equally between rounds, and we also have to take time for breaks into account.
In addition, in each round we have dedicated time for practice and for feedback.

This is simple math for distributing time, but I made a small app to help us do it.
We can set the total session time, and the app will set the time for each round; then we can launch timers with the set time for each round.
As the session goes on, the app recalculates the round for the remaining time to account for any delays.
I haven't used the app yet, but I will this fall as I will be an assistant in a training.
